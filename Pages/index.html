Common Inquiries

How long do terracotta roof tiles typically last? expand_more
Our premium clay tiles are designed to last 75 to 100 years. Unlike asphalt shingles, they do not rot, curl, or degrade under UV exposure, making them a one-time investment for your property.
Are clay tiles too heavy for a standard roof structure? expand_more
While heavier than some alternatives, modern engineering allows most new structures to accommodate them easily. For retrofitting, a minor structural reinforcement might be required.
How do these tiles perform in high-wind regions? expand_more
Our tiles are wind-tunnel tested and certified for Hurricane-Prone Regions (HPR). The weight and locking mechanisms ensure they remain secure even in winds exceeding 150 mph.
